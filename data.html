Datasets

MIKA contains a few datasets generated from publically available data, including ASRS, NTSB, SAFECOM, SAFENET, and the ICS-209-PLUS. Note that all of these datasets are modified formats from the original public data. The data is provided as is with no warrenty or guarantees.
